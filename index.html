My Mission

Kate's Couture is where your favorite pieces find new life, and your vision for everyday wear becomes a reality. I create clothing that isn't just worn, but felt – making you comfortable, beautiful, powerful, and uniquely you.

My Services

Cloning Clothes

We all have that perfectly fitting piece—the one that feels like a second skin, but a tear or stain on night one leaves you heartbroken. Don't give up on it! If I can't mend it, I can clone the entire piece for you. Imagine having four pairs of your favorite, go-to pants, so you never have to worry about wearing them out.

Beyond everyday wear, I can also help preserve cherished memories. If you have a special shirt from a loved one that's becoming delicate, bring it to me. I'll create a new replica, allowing you to keep the original safe while still enjoying your special connection.

Creating New Designs

Do you have a dream outfit in mind but struggle to put it into words? No problem! I'm here to help bring your vision to life, even if it's just a spark of an idea. We'll work together, collaborating every step of the way to design something truly special.

With Kate's Couture, you get a 100% custom fit, crafted specifically for you. You'll also have the exciting opportunity to pick your own fabrics, ensuring your new piece is exactly what you envisioned. To make things even easier, you can visit my dedicated Fitting Guide page (link coming soon!) for simple instructions on how to take your measurements at home. I'm even planning an app to make this process even smoother!

Please note: For high-fashion, formal, or elaborate gown designs, I invite you to explore my "Susciter" page, dedicated to custom couture creations. (Link to Susciter page will go here when created)

Repairs & Alterations

Don't let a small flaw or a changing fit mean the end for your most-loved garments. That favorite club top with a little stain, or a cherished shirt with a minor tear, doesn't have to be retired. I can often work magic with decorative stitches to cover small imperfections, or simply take in a seam to give you that perfect fit back.

I believe in giving your clothes a second chance, ensuring they continue to make you feel comfortable and confident. From quick fixes to more thoughtful adjustments, I can help you fall in love with your wardrobe all over again. Remember that favorite vest that's just a bit too tight? Instead of letting it sit in the closet, I can replace a back panel to give it new life, ensuring you can wear it with joy once more.
